    • Steps

    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti noodles, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
    2. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef until brown. Add mushrooms, onion, garlic, and oregano; saute for 6 minutes. Pour in tomato sauce and tomato paste. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    3. Combine spaghetti noodles with meat sauce. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
